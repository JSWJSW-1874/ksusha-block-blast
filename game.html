📖 HOW TO PLAY

  • 🟦You get 3 shaped pieces at the bottom. Drag and drop them onto the board.
  • ➡️Fill an entire row or column with blocks and it clears — just like magic!
  • 💥Clear 2 or more lines at once for a big bonus — the more you clear together, the more points you get!
  • 🔥Keep clearing lines every move to build a COMBO. Your combo multiplier grows the longer your streak!
  • ⭐Empty the whole board for a PERFECT CLEAR and a massive bonus!
  • 📈Score enough points to level up. Higher levels multiply everything you earn!
  • 💀The game ends when none of the 3 pieces can fit anywhere on the board. Plan ahead!
